Ugali Recipe


Ingredients and steps of Cooking Ugali

Ingredients and tools


  • Maize Flour
  • Water
  • Cooking Stick
  • Sufuria
  • Source of heat
  • Placing Plate
  • Cooking Oil

Steps of Cooking Ugali

  1. Place sufuria on source of heat
  2. Add water to the sufuria
  3. Heat water to Boiling point of about 100 degrees Celcius
  4. Add cooking oil
  5. Add at least 2 cups of Maize flour
  6. Start Stirring a paste is formed
  7. Continue adding flour till paste hardnes
  8. Start Shapping to desired shape
  9. Leave for approximately 10 minutes before turning
  10. When ready overturn it onto a plate
  11. Serve with Desired Stew
